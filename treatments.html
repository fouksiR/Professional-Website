Fertility Services

Comprehensive, evidence-based reproductive care tailored to your individual needs and goals.

Getting Started

Fertility Assessment

A comprehensive, personalised review of your fertility goals, medical background, and reproductive health. Includes hormone testing, pelvic ultrasound, semen analysis, and a tailored plan for your journey ahead.

Natural Cycle Tracking

Ultrasound and blood test monitoring to identify ovulation timing and your most fertile window — supporting natural conception without medical intervention.

Ovulation Induction

Medication-assisted stimulation for irregular or absent ovulation. Closely monitored with ultrasound and blood tests to optimise timing and maximise pregnancy chances.

Intrauterine Insemination

A minimally invasive procedure placing prepared sperm directly into the uterus at ovulation. Often combined with ovulation induction to improve success rates.

Fertility Preservation

Egg Freezing

Oocyte cryopreservation for elective or medical reasons. Preserves fertility potential for future use — with comprehensive counselling on timing, success rates, and what to expect.

Sperm Freezing

Sperm cryopreservation before medical treatment, surgery, or for personal planning. Frozen sperm can be used in IUI or IVF when you're ready.

Assisted Reproduction

IVF / ICSI

In vitro fertilisation with or without intracytoplasmic sperm injection. Laboratory fertilisation and embryo transfer — personalised protocols for optimal outcomes.

Donor Conception & Surrogacy

Inclusive pathways to parenthood using donor sperm, donor eggs, or surrogacy. Individualised care for single individuals, same-sex couples, and transgender patients.

Women's Health

Early Pregnancy Scanning

Ultrasound at 6–8 weeks to confirm intrauterine pregnancy and fetal heartbeat. Includes early pregnancy guidance on lifestyle, nutrition, and next steps.

PCOS Management

Long-term management of polycystic ovary syndrome outside of fertility — addressing irregular cycles, metabolic health, and reducing cardiovascular and diabetes risk.

Endometriosis & Fertility

Pre-surgical fertility evaluation including ovarian reserve testing and imaging. Ensures surgical planning aligns with your reproductive goals and preserves fertility potential.

Menopause

Symptom management and long-term health planning through perimenopause and menopause. Personalised hormonal and non-hormonal treatment options.

Specialised Care

Male Infertility

Comprehensive evaluation of hormonal, genetic, and lifestyle factors affecting sperm. Integrated management including lifestyle optimisation, medical treatment, and assisted reproduction.

Complex Reproductive Endocrinology

Advanced care for challenging hormonal conditions — premature ovarian insufficiency, hypothalamic amenorrhoea, resistant PCOS, and complex menopausal presentations.
